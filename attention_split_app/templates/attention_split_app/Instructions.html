The task is intentionally repetitive. You will see a mostly static screen. At unpredictable times, a small pop-up will appear with an easy prompt (for example, “Press F” or “Press J,” or a very simple question). The prompts are designed so that performance depends primarily on paying attention, not on skill.

We will record your performance. Your outcome is based on your accuracy rate: the proportion of prompts you answer correctly. After finishing both attention tasks sessions, you will receive a payment that is 50 dollars times your overall accuracy rate. For example, if your overall accuracy rate is 0.8, you will receive 0.8 × 50 = 40 dollars.
